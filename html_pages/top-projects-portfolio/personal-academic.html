My Role

This project is a personal academic performance dashboard that was inspired by Kelly Adams. The dashboard tracks my academic performance by extracting data from the Future University in Egypt student portal into Excel and then creating a Tableau dashboard.

The data for the dashboard was extracted from the Future University in Egypt student portal. The data includes my grades, attendance, and participation in extracurricular activities. The data was exported from the portal as a CSV file and then imported into Excel

My academic journey has been a unique mix of passion and hustle. Running my own side businesses during college's first two years kept me fully absorbed, leaving little room for academic focus. However, I am now committed to improving my academic performance and reaching my goals. I believe that by tracking my progress and making changes to my study habits, I can achieve my desired results.

The data was analyzed in Tableau to create a dashboard that tracks my academic performance. The dashboard includes charts and graphs that show my grades, attendance, and study hours over time. The dashboard also includes filters that allow me to view the data by semester, course, or instructor.

The results of this project are a personal academic performance dashboard that tracks my progress and helps me identify areas where I can improve. I am confident that by using this dashboard, I can reach my academic goals.

I plan to continue using the dashboard to track my progress and make changes to my study habits. I also plan to add additional data to the dashboard, such as my attendance. I believe that the dashboard will be a valuable tool that helps me reach my academic goals.
