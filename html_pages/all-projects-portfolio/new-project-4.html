HR Attrition Dashboard

Description

The dashboard is designed to help HR managers identify trends in attrition and take steps to mitigate them.

Tools Used

Excel

Kaggle




My Role

Employee turnover is a costly problem for businesses of all sizes. In fact, the cost of replacing a single employee can be as much as 213% of their annual salary. That's why it's so important to understand why employees are leaving your company and take steps to address the problem.

Secondly, the project begins by sourcing data from platforms like Kaggle. This dataset serves as the foundation for exploring and analyzing the factors contributing to attrition.

Thirdly, employing advanced data cleaning techniques, the collected dataset undergoes meticulous scrutiny. Corrections for data types and any inconsistencies are made to ensure the highest data integrity and reliability. This step lays the groundwork for accurate analysis.

Fourthly, once the data is clean, it can be analyzed to identify trends in employee attrition. This analysis can help you to understand the reasons why employees are leaving your company, such as low pay, poor working conditions, or lack of opportunities for advancement. For this project, the data was analyzed using pivot tables in Excel. This allowed me to identify trends in employee attrition by employee demographics, job title, department, tenure with the company, and reasons for leaving the company.

Fifthly, The power of visualization comes into play as the dashboard presents the data in captivating and easily understandable charts. Gender distribution, attrition versus employee education, department-wise attrition, attrition by tenure and environment satisfaction, attrition by overtime and marital status, attrition by job role, and even multi-dimensional business travel attrition are skillfully depicted through intuitive bar, donut, and other chart formats.

The dashboard incorporates vital Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) essential for a comprehensive examination of attrition. Key metrics such as employee count, attrition rate, average age, and more are calculated and visualized, providing a holistic view of the attrition landscape.
